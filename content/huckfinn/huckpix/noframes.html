Huck Finn Illustrations

In this section any of the 174 Kemble illustrations in the novel's first American edition can be accessed through the list below, where they are listed by their captions.

Many of the illustrations can also be viewed another way. 65 of them were on pages used in the Sales Prospectus for Huck Finn. These are indicated below by a (*). By clicking on the (*), you can see the full page on which the illustration appeared.
