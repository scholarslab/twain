UVA MT Print Collection

[The University of Virginia's Department of Special Collections has several dozen photographic images of MT in its "Prints File." The 28 photos below are a sampling of them. They are a miscellaneous group, which includes photos posed and shot by professionals in studios around the world, candids taken by friends with early Kodaks, photos reproduced in newspaper articles and a hand-colored postcard. I've tried to arrange them chronologically, but only a few are clearly dated, so most of these dates are approximate.]
