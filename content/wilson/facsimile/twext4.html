introduction

While David Wilson's election as a Pudd'nhead certainly usurps his legal career within the community of Dawson's Landing, the tradition of Southern Honor supplants legal statutes with dueling. The feudal code of dueling represents "an unwritten law" to which even Wilson, a New Yorker by birth, eventually subscribes. When Tom Driscoll brings his assault case before the court, Wilson says "the thing that puzzled me was, why [Percy Driscoll] let you carry such a matter into a court of law at all, either before the duel or after it. It's no place for it."
