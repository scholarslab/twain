Available here are all 432 pages of the American Publishing Company edition of The Tragedy of Pudd'nhead Wilson and the Comedy of Those Extraordinary Twins. The scans were made from a 1900 reprinting of the first (1894) American edition. Reading or looking at the novel this way allows you experience MT's text as contemporary American readers did, with the hundreds of "marginal illustrations" (see ILLUSTRATING PUDD'NHEAD).
    The copy we scanned came from the open stacks of Alderman Library, so you will also see several dozen marginal comments and underlinings made by an unknown reader sometime between 1900 and 1997.
