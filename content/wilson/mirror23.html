The Venerable Henry Howe III–Notes and News fo Current Attractions.

[Special to The Mirror]

The appearance of Frank Mayo in Pudd'nhead Wilson has been patiently awaited by the patrons of the New Grand, and his opening there to-night was in the nature of an ovation. The house was crowded.

The Walnut Street welcomed Frank Mayo in Pudd'nhead Wilson with an enthusiastic audience. Mark Twain's romantic story has been woven into a play that appeals to the hearts of all. The supporting company was an excellent one. Underlined is Minnie Maddern Fiske.

Dramatic Mirror 29 February 1896
