BUTTE.--ITEMS: Mr. William L. Malley, manager of the Pudd'nhead Wilson co., has made Butte his headquarters for this part of his co.'s Northwestern tour, and has spent some two weeks in the city. . . .

Dramatic Mirror 3 December 1898

BUTTE.--GRAND OPERA HOUSE (G. O. McFarland, manager): . . . Edwin Mayo in Pudd'nhead Wilson 24-26; performance especially good, and evening of 24 every seat in the house was filled and many stood. Edwin Mayo was well received and the entire co. was good, especially W. R. McVey and Ada Dwyer.

Dramatic Mirror 10 December 1898
