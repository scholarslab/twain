LEADVILLE.--WESTON OPERA HOUSE (A. S. Weston, manager): Frank Mayo, supported by a good co., in Pudd'nhead Wilson May 28 to fair business at advanced rates.

PUEBLO.–GRAND OPERA HOUSE (S. N. Nve, manager): Pudd'nhead Wilson May 29 to good business; audience delighted.

Dramatic Mirror 13 June 1896
