ATLANTA

. . . Pudd'nhead Wilson will play a return engagement at the Lyceum 7-8. This co., headed by Theodore Hamilton and Joseph Jefferson, Jr., scored a big hit on their former appearance here.

Dramatic Mirror 12 March 1898

ATLANTA

. . . Pudd'nhead Wilson was the bill 7, 8. This co., headed by Theodore Hamilton and Joseph Jefferson, Jr., drew three large houses at the Lyceum, although it had been here once before this season. It is one of the best on the road and pleases everybody.

Dramatic Mirror 19 March 1898
