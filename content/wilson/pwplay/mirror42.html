BROOKLYN THEATRES.
Park.--Pudd'nhead Wilson.

Theodore Hamilton's fine rendition of the title role of Mark Twain's impressive story was in evidence for the second time this season on Monday. Since its last visit several changes of cast have been made. Arnold Daly's delightful presentation of Chambers, the supposed slave boy, is particularly missed. The popular comedian, Joseph Hart, in his latest venture, The Tarrytown Widow, is Palmer and Knowles's next underline.

Dramatic Mirror 10 April 1897
