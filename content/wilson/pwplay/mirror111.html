SAGINAW.--ACADEMY OF MUSIC (J. H. Davidson, manager): Frank [sic] Mayo in Pudd'nhead Wilson 14; fair house. Walter Campau [sic], of this co., was taken ill while on the stage and had to be removed to his hotel. He was compelled to remain here several days, but was able to rejoin his co. 16.

Dramatic Mirror 29 October 1898
