SAN DIEGO.--FISHER OPERA HOUSE (John C. Fisher, manager): . . . Edwin Mayo 4 in Pudd'nhead Wilson. Mr. Mayo being ill, his part was taken by William S. Gill, who, upon only twenty-four hours' notice and with no previous study, acquitted himself admirably. Ada Dwyer and Frank Campau scored heavily.

Dramatic Mirror 28 January 1899
