MARION.--THE GRAND (E. L. Kinneman, manager): . . . Edwin Mayo and his excellent co. presented Pudd'nhead Wilson to a large and appreciative audience 26. Mr. Mayo in the name part gives an intelligent interpretation. The Rowey of Bess Maguire was chaming. Frank Campeau, William R. McKay, Menifee Johnstone, Frances Mayo, and Belle Stoddard deserve much praise. The staging is complete in all details.

Dramatic Mirror 10 February 1900
