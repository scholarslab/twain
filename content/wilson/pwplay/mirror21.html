CLEVELAND.

Comedy to suit all tastes is the rule at the playhouses to-night. At the Euclid, that starling actor Frank mayo is seem in the title role of his dramatization of Pudd'nhead Wilson. He has a strong supporting company. The Euclid Avenue Opera House has its usual representative Monday night audience. The play seems to give satisfaction, and will undoubtedly do a flattering business. 1492 comes next week, followed by Olga Nethersole in Carmen.

Dramatic Mirror 15 February 1896
