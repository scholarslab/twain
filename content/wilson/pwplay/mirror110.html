GRAND RAPIDS.--POWER'S (O. Stair, manager): Pudd'nhead Wilson is indeed a welcome visitor, as was attested 10-15, when large audiences enjoyed its drolleries and applauded the homely scenes and situations in which the play abounds. Edwin F. Mayo's Pudd'nhead in the same lovable fellow Mark Twain pictured, and the star's portrayal throughout is a delicately shaded, artistic performance.

Dramatic Mirror 29 October 1898
