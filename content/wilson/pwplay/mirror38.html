WASHINGTON.

Pudd'nhead Wilson, in the title-role of which Theodore Hamilton makes a conspicuous success, opened to a very excellent house at Rapley's New National Theatre. Many of the original cast were seen in the presentation, including Eleanor Morrett, Frances Graham, Lucille La Verne, Frank B. Aiken, Newton Chisnell, Frank currier, Frank Champeau, Arnold Daly, W. S. Gill, and J. P. Tucker. Francis Wilson in Half a King comes comes next.

Dramatic Mirror 5 December 1896
