HARTFORD.–PROCTOR'S OPERA HOUSE (F. W. Lloyd Manager):

The first performance of Mark Twain's story, "Pudd'nhead Wilson," dramatized by Frank Mayo, occurred 8, 9 and as chronicled in the special dispatch on the opening night, made a great sucess. On the second presentation much of the dialogue had been cut out, making the play run smoother.

Dramatic Mirror 20 April 1895
