Picturing Those "Old Times"

  "Old Times on the Mississippi" originally appeared in 1876 in The Atlantic Magazine in seven monthly installments -- without illustrations. The piece was published again as 14 chapters in Life on the Mississippi (1883). Life was a subscription book, and so "lavishly" illustrated by three different artists.
