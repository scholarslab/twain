When the slaves on "Marse George"'s plantation learn he's going to marry "Miss Charlotte," they ask for a chance to "pay dee bespects to her." That's what is happening here. One of the slaves, Charley Brown, "meck her a pretty speech, an' tell her we mighty proud to own her."
