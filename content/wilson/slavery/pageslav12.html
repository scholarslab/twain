This is one of the illustrations that C. S. Reinhart drew in 1893 for a giftbook publication of "Meh Lady." It's narrator is simply referred to as "uncle," and he always refers to its heroine as "Meh Lady." It's mainly a war-time tale of the courage of the women of old Virginia, who resist the Yankees with their own weapons -- grace and charm, but mainly pride and a sense of their own obligations to themselves and their dependents.
