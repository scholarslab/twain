Like Harris' first Uncle Remus book, this one contains a section of "His Songs and Ballads." The illustration here accompanies the song "Oh, Gimme de Gal!": "Oh, de strappin' black gal, de big greasy gal! | She kyar herself mighty fine! | How de boys gwineter foller along in de row, | A-waitin' fer ter ketch her sign?"
