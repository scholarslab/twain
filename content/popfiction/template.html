Essay on Reading

This is the template to follow when e-mailing me your essay. The unitalicized stuff below is the html tagging. Just copy exactly what's here, replacing the italicized stuff with the particulars of your essay. Then e-mail it to me at sfr@virginia.edu. Please send it in the body of an e-mail message, NOT as an attachment. This means that if you typed it first in a word-processing program like WordPerfect or MicrosoftWord, you'll have to convert it to an ASCII or DOS or TEXT file in order to include it successfully in an e-mail message. If you're not sure how to make that conversion (you use the "Save As" filetype program in your word processor), just let me know.
