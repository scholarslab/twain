Mark Twain to Augustin Daly, 1884 Feb 17

Feb. 17/84.

Dear Mr. Daly —

I have been dra-
matizing a book of
mine ("The Adventures
of Tom Sawyer") & I
wonder if you would
like to take a look at
the result, with an eye
to business? If so,
I will bring the play down when I
return to New York Wednesday Thursday.

Yr truly

Mark Twain
