Mark Twain, Riverdale, NY, to Unknown (1901-1903)

Riverdale
on the Hudson, New York City —
Tuesday morning

The bearer is my daughter's
maid, & I beg as a favor
that you will allow her to
have access to my daughter's
room, so that she can
unpack the trunk.

Yours very truly

Mark Twain
(SL. )

My daughter
is Miss Clara
Clemens.
