Mark Twain to Unknown; on verso Mark Twain to Charles Erskine Scott Wood with AN by Charles Erskine Scott WOod, 1882 Aug

OVER

Hartford, Wednesday.

Dear Sir

In reply I am obliged
to say that I have quitted the platform
permanently. With thanks for the
complement of your invitation
I am

Truly Yours

Mark Twain
