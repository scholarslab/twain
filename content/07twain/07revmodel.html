Review of Secondary Source

Between the lines below is the template to follow when e-mailing me your reviews. The unitalicized stuff below is the html tagging. Just copy exactly what's here, replacing the italicized stuff with the particulars of your review. Then e-mail it to me at sfr@virginia.edu. Please send it in the body of an e-mail message, NOT as an attachment. This means that if you typed it first in a word-processing program like WordPerfect or MicrosoftWord, you'll have to convert it to a .txt file in order to include it successfully in an e-mail message. If you're not sure how to make that conversion (you use the "Save As" filetype program in your word processor), just let me know. Once you've converted the review to a .txt file, copy and paste it into an e-mail message. Make sure you proofread the review before you hit the SEND button. Check, for example, that details like quotation marks, apostrophes, and dashes display correctly in .txt format.
