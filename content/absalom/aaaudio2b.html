     Q.   And he never really attained this respectability?

    A.   No, he was -- the Greeks destroyed him, the old Greek concept of tragedy. He wanted a son which symbolized this ideal, and he got too many sons -- his sons destroyed one another and then him. He was left with -- the only son he had left was a Negro.
