"So she [Judith] did not tell him [Sutpen] where Henry and Bon were and he did not discover it until after the University company departed, because Bon and Henry enrolled and then hid themselves somewhere. They must have; they must have paused in Oxford only long enough to enroll before riding on, because no one who knew them either in Oxford or Jefferson knew that they were members of the company at the time, which would have been almost impossible to conceal otherwise. (p. 97)
