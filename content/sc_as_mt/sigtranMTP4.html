It seems unnatural not to see you this morning, you precious girl -- & it seems odd not to find Mrs Langdon's [face] among the faces about me, or hear the pleasant cackle of that absurd cousin of yours -- I send her my very kindest regards. Give Mr. Langdon my love, please -- he is at home by this time.

With a fervent blessing, & a prayer for you, & many & many a kiss, my dear Livy,

Sam[l. L. C.]
