MT as Clemens & Twain

This form of signature may have been decided on by the publishers of the various special signed editions that were sold during MT's lifetime; when he writes the two names together on a letter or elsewhere he tends to keep them at least slightly apart. When he uses this overlapping form, he usually does let "Mark Twain" cover "S.L. Clemens." This edition of Following the Equator was brought out by the American Publishing Company in 1896.
