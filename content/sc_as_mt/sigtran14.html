Farmington Avenue, Hartford.
Dec. 1./74.

My Dear Miss Street:

I remember your father very well indeed. His courtesies to us in Salt Lake City were of so pleasant a nature that even the fourteen years that have rolled by since have not sufficed to obliterate the memory of them.

And now after all this time I am to repay that debt with the base coin of an assumed name! But you have asked it, & so--as the Persians say--on your head be it!

Very Truly Yours
Sam.l L. Clemens
(alias) Mark Twain
