[From The Chicago Daily Tribune, 15 January 1885]

MARK TWAIN'S LATEST WORK.

  BOSTON, Mass., Jan. 14.–The suit of Samuel Clemens against Estes & Lauriat, publishers, to restrain the latter from offering the last work of Clemens at less than the subscription price, came up in the United States Circuit Court to-day. Estes & Lauriat said they would cancel the catalog which made the offer, although they felt bound to carry out the contracts already made, which involved only thirty-two copies of the work.
