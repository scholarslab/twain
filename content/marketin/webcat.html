Webster & Co. Catalogue

In 1892 MT's publishing company had so many books on its list that its illustrated catalogue ran to over 40 pages. The MT titles it owned took up seven of them. Connecticut Yankee is on the first two pages listed below (after the cover); Huck on the third; Life on the Mississippi, the fourth; MT's Library of Humor, the fourth and fifth; The American Claimant, the sixth; and The Prince and the Pauper on the last. Included are ads for several "cheap editions" of these texts, and a special, boxed, three-volume "Holiday Set."
